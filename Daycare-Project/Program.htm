Program & Curriculum

Infant Class (6 Weeks - 15 Months)

Our Infant program allows babies to grow and learn every day. We offer a safe and nurturing home away from home where children can explore and make new discoveries. Children learn through play and our program is designed to provide infants with the skills that serve as building blocks for a lifetime of learning. We are committed to making this first transition away from home easy and natural for you and your child. We offer daily reports so you can keep track of your child’s day here at the center. Our daily infant reports include complete daily feeding and diapering history.

Toddler Class (16 Months - 3 Years)

Our Toddler program helps children build friendships, self-esteem, and curiosity. Our staff will assist them to explore their world in a safe and nurturing environment. Each day is filled with fun activities that excite and help children develop in ways that give them the comfort and confidence to grow and develop both cognitively and socially. We offer daily reports so you can keep track of your child’s day here at the center. We utilize 123 Learn (Parent Aware approved) curriculum to lay a foundation for learning letters, numbers and colors.

Pre - Kindergarten Class (3 Years - 5 Years)

Our Pre-K program helps children gain more self-esteem, so they feel ready to take on the world. Our Pre-K program enhances that confidence by providing activities to help children become problem solvers and develop a love of lifelong learning. This is a time when children are making connections between what they know and what they are learning. Through independent exploration, structured activities, and hands-on learning, children develop a variety of skills and knowledge. They learn to participate in routines, make decisions, and planning techniques. We help prepare your child for Kindergarten in a private or public setting. We offer daily reports so you can keep track of your child’s day here at the center. We use 123 Learn (Parent Aware approved) curriculum and other tools to teach letters, numbers and to prepare them for reading and basic math.
