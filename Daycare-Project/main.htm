Welcome to God's Little Treasures

Hello and welcome to God's Little Treasures! We are a Christian preschool and childcare center located in Brooklyn Center, MN. We provide children from 6 weeks to 5 years old with a safe, loving, fun and nurturing environment in an educational faith based setting.
Children learn and grow through play, study, music and prayer and prepare for the challenges and opportunities that await them as they embark on their exciting journey through life on God's planet Earth. We are licensed by the Minnesota Department of Human Services (DHS) and meet or exceed all state standards and regulations for child care centers. Our staff has many years of experience in early childhood education and we maintain small group sizes to provide more personal attention to the needs of every child in our care.
